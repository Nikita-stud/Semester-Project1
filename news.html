Moon Eclipse

An event that happens once in a century is coming up in January. Eclipse is something only seen in movies or read about in books for the most of us.

This time you will be able to witness an eclipse with your own eyes. Come to the Museum in good time to get to lend the best Telescope and place it on the grounds of outside area of the museum.

Loan of Skeletons

Come and see the huge amount of new species never seen before at Community Science Museum. Our museum will be hosting an exhibition of skeletons of dinosaurs species that have long disappeared. You will be able to see Stegosaurus, Ankylosaurus, Brontosaurus and many other species that lived on the planet millions of years before human beings.

1 January - 1 July

Information for teachers

As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring. Our team are on hand to give your students guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning laboratory, and provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them.

Please get in touch with us here to find out more about facillities and to arrange a time to bring your students for a visit.

Information for Kids

During the school holidays we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children your age on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. To find out more about the holiday club and how you can join, send us a message.
